OSCD Sprint #2: Backlog


This time we decided to detail backlog to bite-size tasks with Estimated Time to Complete (ETC) around 15-30 minutes each. Also, we listed the knowledge required to solve them. You will find these tasks in the GitHub issues listed in the tables below.

The backlog does not limit contributions. You are welcome to contribute your private analytics (Atomic Tests, Sigma Rules, TheHive Responders) that are outside of the defined scope.

Required Knowledge

Regardless of the topic you would like to work on, you will need to get yourself familiar with the GitHub flow. Please refer to the OSCD How-To to get yourself fully prepared for the Sprint.
